The Urban Pinholer

...lenses are for whimps

In this age of digital dominance we face a real dilema in photography. The camera has never been more accessible, more omnipresent; the image has never been more transient, more disposable. What we have gained in instantaneous gratification and automation of technique and technology, we have perhaps lost in focus and the pure pleasure of craft

 

The Urban Pinholer's aim is to re-invent the camera ritual, to rediscover the art of photography through the purity, the simplicity of the pinhole camera
